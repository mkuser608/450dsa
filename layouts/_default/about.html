About 450 DSA Cracker

What is 450 DSA Cracker?

450 DSA Cracker is a curated list of 450 Data Structures and Algorithms problems designed to help you ace your coding interviews. This list was created by Love Babbar and has helped thousands of students prepare for technical interviews.

The problems are organized by topic, covering all the essential areas of DSA that are commonly asked in interviews at top tech companies like Google, Amazon, Microsoft, and more.

This website provides a structured way to track your progress as you solve these problems, with features to mark problems as completed, bookmark important ones, and add personal notes.

Features

  • Progress Tracking: Mark problems as completed and see your overall progress.
  • Bookmarks: Save important problems to revisit later.
  • Notes: Add personal notes to problems for future reference.
  • Search: Quickly find problems by name or content.
  • Dark Mode: Switch between light and dark themes for comfortable viewing.
  • Cloud Sync: Optionally sign in to sync your progress across devices.
  • Leaderboard: See how your progress compares to others in the community.

How to Use

  1. Browse Topics: Start by exploring the different DSA topics on the home page.
  2. Select a Topic: Click on a topic to see all the problems in that category.
  3. Solve Problems: Click on the problem links to access the problem on platforms like GeeksForGeeks or Coding Ninjas.
  4. Track Progress: After solving a problem, mark it as completed by checking the box next to it.
  5. Add Notes: Click the notes icon to add personal notes about your approach or things to remember.
  6. Bookmark: Use the bookmark icon to save important problems for later review.
  7. Create Account (Optional): Sign in with Google or GitHub to sync your progress across devices and appear on the leaderboard.

New Features

Account System

Create an account to sync your progress across devices. Sign in with Google or GitHub for a seamless experience. Your local progress is preserved and merged with cloud data when you sign in.

Leaderboard

Compare your progress with other users on the leaderboard. See who has solved the most problems and where you rank in the community. You can opt out of the leaderboard in your account settings if you prefer.

Data Synchronization

Your progress is automatically synced between your devices when you're signed in. You can also manually sync or export your data from the account page.
